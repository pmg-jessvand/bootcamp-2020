Lowki
Enhance your bike experience

Lowki is a safe electric bike lock with a lot of built-in handy features.

Contribute to this project Watch promo video

No key, no hassle

With Lowki you don't need a key to open your lock. You can just tap it with your phone or open the app and push the button

Geolocation implementation

Find your bike anywhere in cowded area's. You can also find the nearest parkingspaces for your bike and let your friends know where your are

Analyse

The Lowki app can give you a update of the state of your bike. Keep your bike in excellent condition
